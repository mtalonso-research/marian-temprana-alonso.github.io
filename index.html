Marian Temprana Alonso

Bio

Marian Temprana Alonso is a PhD student in the Π-CoLab group at the Knight Foundation School of Computing and Information Sciences, Florida International University. She is also a graduate research intern at Sandia National Laboratories. Marian earned her BSc in Applied Mathematics from Florida International University in 2022. Her research is centered on advancing energy-efficient communications, data compression, and sensing.

Research Interests

  • Data Compression
  • Wireless Communications
  • Sensing
